Battery connector

To make your on-board wiring work well, you have to hunt down every possible voltage drop. SureConnect 4 takes care of the battery connection. By use of a cable with crimped cable-shoes it is possible to get a gas-tight connection with almost no voltage drop. All the cable strands will be connected resulting in a very low resistance and a very low power loss. It is easy to make a jumper to the second battery, to connect a VHF or telephone equipment. There is sufficent space for up to 4 separate connections directly on the terminal, all with the same low voltage drop.
